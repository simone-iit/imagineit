About Us

Breaking into tech shouldn’t feel impossible. At Imagine IT, we believe in creating real-world opportunities for aspiring developers and technologists through our University Partnership Program.

We partner with universities to give students and recent graduates the chance to gain professional experience that truly matters. Our program isn’t about busy work — participants get to work on actual projects that help solve real problems for real clients.

You'll apply just like you would for any job — and that means you’ll be considered like a real candidate, not rejected by a bot before your story is heard. Every applicant is reviewed by a human, and every opportunity is a chance to make a meaningful impact.

Whether you’re building your first portfolio or hoping to transition into the tech industry, we’re here to support your journey and match you with opportunities that matter.
